Modes of Travel in Himachal Pradesh

Train Travel

Traveling by train is a scenic way to explore the beautiful landscapes of Himachal Pradesh. You can book your tickets online through the official IRCTC website.

Bus Travel

Buses are a popular option for intercity travel. You can find various private and state-operated buses connecting major cities in Himachal Pradesh.

Use RedBus for easy bus ticket booking.

Flight Travel

Flights are the quickest way to reach Himachal Pradesh. Major airports include Gaggal Airport in Dharamshala and Jubbarhatti Airport in Shimla.

Various airlines offer flights to these destinations.
